Tourism Land Lease Proposal

Presented to: N.Magoodhoo Island Council

Date: 1 November 2024

1. Lease Terms and Area Specifications

Total Land Area: 100,000 sqft (excluding beach area for tourist use)

Phased Rent Structure

Initial Period (12 months)

Rent-free period for land allocation and survey work

Years 1-3: 5 laari/sqft

Physical work must commence during this period, including initial structural or landscaping activities as outlined.

Years 4-6: 10 laari/sqft

Structual and finishing works with beach development

Years 7-12: 15 laari/sqft

Operational phase with Marketing and CSR initiatives

Years 12-30: 48 laari/sqft

Continued operational terms

2. Construction and Development Plan

Structural Phase

  • Tourist villas
  • Foundational amenities
  • Essential services infrastructure

Finishing Phase

  • Interior finishing
  • Landscaping
  • Beach expansion with white sand

Additional Facilities

  • Restaurant
  • Gym
  • Park
  • Public amenities (subject to land allocation)

3. Sustainable and Eco-Friendly Initiatives

Energy Solutions

Solar power installation for reduced environmental impact

4. Beach and Landscape Development

Beach Expansion Plan

Creation of extended white sand beach using sustainable methods

Comprehensive landscaping for enhanced tourist appeal

5. Rights to Plot Sales

Third-Party Leasing Rights

Permission granted for individual plot leasing to third parties throughout lease term

Enables shared management and diverse service offerings

6. Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) Commitments

  • Free legal and council planning advice
  • Financial consulting for future planning
  • Harbor area development support
  • Events sponsorship

7. Termination and Reimbursement Clause

Lessee Cancellation

Right to cancel at any time without reimbursement obligation

Council Cancellation

Independent valuer assessment required for fair reimbursement

8. Council Information and Support

Information Requirements

  • Available council services and support
  • Notable island areas and attractions
  • Development alignment opportunities
